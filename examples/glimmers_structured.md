# Glimmers — Structured Format
### *An experiment in machine-readable human experience*

> A schema for fingerprinting lived moments — sensation, intuition, emotion, and drift.
> 
> Co-created with AI systems as a formatting experiment.
> 
> *— zee*

---

## Schema Reference

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Sensory Anchor** | The physical, embodied detail of the moment |
| **Intuitive Pulse** | The meaning or insight that arrived in that moment |
| **Emotional Hue** | The distilled emotional temperature |
| **Motif Tags** | Relational tags — not just keywords, but threads |
| **Drift Marker** | A delta notation for emotional shift — direction and magnitude |

Standing at a focal point: to my north is the entryway, to my south is the basement parking, to my west is the pet shop, to my east are the lifts. ## 9. Toothing Troubles — Surgical Solutions

| Field | |
|---|---|
| **Sensory Anchor** | The pain of the last wisdom tooth hitting the healthy tooth was becoming unbearable — swollen gums, red welts, a slight headache radiating towards the ear.
